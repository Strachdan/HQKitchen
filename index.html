About HQ Kitchen

From the kitchen at HQ we escaped the daily monotony of lockdown by baking. We dug out old recipes and dared to challenge ourselves to new ones. Eventually we were under a pile of food and had to share. This led us down an exciting path and we want to share our baking with you.
